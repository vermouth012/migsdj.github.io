My Kind of Film

Favorite TV Shows

How I Met Your Mother

Genre: Sitcom

Director: Pamela Fryman

Stars: Josh Radnor, Neil Patrick Harris, Cobie Smulders

Ted Mosby recounts to his children the story of how he met their mother, taking them through a series of hilarious and heartwarming adventures with his friends Marshall, Lily, Barney, and Robin in New York City. The journey spans nine seasons, filled with love, friendship, and unexpected twists.

Stranger Things

Genre: Science-fiction, Horror, Mystery, Drama

Director: The Duffer Brothers

Stars: Millie Bobby Brown, Finn Wolfhard, Winona Ryder

Set in the 1980s, Stranger Things follows a group of kids in the small town of Hawkins, Indiana, as they encounter a young girl with supernatural abilities and uncover a sinister government experiment. As they search for their missing friend, they face terrifying creatures from the Upside Down that threaten their world.

Favorite Movies

A Walk to Remember

Genre: Romance, Drama

Director: Adam Shankman

Stars: Mandy Moore, Shane West, Peter Coyote

Landon Carter, a rebellious high school senior, falls in love with Jamie Sullivan, a kind and reserved preacher’s daughter. As their relationship deepens, Landon discovers that Jamie has a heartbreaking secret that changes both of their lives forever.

Dragon Ball Super: Broly

Genre: Action

Director: Tatsuya Nagamine

Stars: Masako Nozawa, Ryô Horikawa, Bin Shimada

Goku and Vegeta face their most powerful opponent yet when they encounter Broly, a Saiyan with immense strength and a mysterious past. As Broly’s power spirals out of control, Goku and his allies must push their limits to protect Earth from total destruction.
