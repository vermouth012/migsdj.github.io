Why Japan Is Special To Me

Japan holds a special place in my heart for many reasons. Since I was young, I’ve been fascinated by Japanese culture, from anime and manga to their unique traditions and customs. The blend of modern technology and rich history makes Japan unlike any other place. I’m drawn to its peaceful temples, vibrant festivals, and delicious food. The idea of walking through bustling Tokyo streets, experiencing the quiet beauty of Kyoto’s shrines, and savoring authentic Japanese cuisine excites me. Visiting Japan would be a dream come true, a chance to immerse myself in a culture I’ve admired for so long.

How to Get There

By Air

Major international airports:

  • Direct flights from Manila (MNL) to Narita International Airport (NRT) or Haneda Airport (HND).
  • Flight duration is approximately 4–5 hours.
  • Airlines include Philippine Airlines, Japan Airlines, and ANA.

Transportation

Once in Japan, travel efficiently using:

  • Japan Rail Pass (JR Pass): Ideal for traveling across multiple cities using Shinkansen (bullet trains).
  • Tokyo Metro: Easy and affordable way to explore Tokyo’s major districts.
  • IC Cards (Suica or Pasmo): Convenient for bus, train, and subway rides across Japan.

Other Must-Visit Places

Osaka

Known as Japan’s “Kitchen,” Osaka offers delicious street food like takoyaki and okonomiyaki. Osaka Castle stands as a historical landmark showcasing Japan’s rich heritage, while Dotonbori is a vibrant district filled with neon lights, entertainment, and amazing local cuisine.

Hokkaido

Famous for its breathtaking winter landscapes and skiing resorts, Hokkaido also offers relaxing hot springs and beautiful flower fields during summer. It’s a perfect escape from the busy city life.
