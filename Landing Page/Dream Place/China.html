Why China Is Special To Me

I want to visit China to explore its beautiful places, experience its rich culture, and visit its historical museums. China’s blend of ancient history and modern innovation is fascinating. From iconic landmarks like the Great Wall to vibrant cities like Hong Kong, there’s so much to discover. I’m also interested in learning more about Chinese traditions and enjoying the warmth of the people there.

How to Get There

By Air

Major international airports:

  • Direct flights from Manila (MNL) to Beijing Capital International Airport (PEK), Shanghai Pudong International Airport (PVG), or Hong Kong International Airport (HKG).
  • Flight duration is around 3–4 hours.
  • Airlines include Philippine Airlines, China Southern, and Cathay Pacific.

Transportation

Once in China, travel efficiently using:

  • High-Speed Trains: Fast and convenient for traveling between major cities.
  • Metro Systems: Available in cities like Beijing, Shanghai, and Hong Kong.
  • Taxis and Ride-Sharing Apps: Ideal for short distances and local travel.

Other Must-Visit Places

Shanghai

Shanghai is a bustling metropolis known for its modern skyline, including the iconic Oriental Pearl Tower. The Bund offers a stunning view of the city’s architectural wonders.

Beijing

Beijing is home to historical treasures like the Forbidden City and the Temple of Heaven. Exploring the city allows visitors to experience China’s imperial history and vibrant culture.
