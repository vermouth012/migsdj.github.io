Breathtaking Sceneries

Venice, with its charming canals and gondolas
Rome, home to ancient landmarks and rich history
Florence, known for its Renaissance art and architecture

Why Italy Is Special To Me

I want to visit Italy to explore its beautiful places, experience its rich culture, and visit its historical museums. Italy offers a perfect mix of ancient wonders, breathtaking landscapes, and delicious cuisine. From the canals of Venice to the ruins of Rome, and the artistic treasures in Florence, there’s so much to discover. I’m also interested in learning more about Italian traditions and meeting the warm and friendly people there.

How to Get There

By Air

Major international airports:

  • Direct or connecting flights from Manila (MNL) to Leonardo da Vinci–Fiumicino Airport (FCO) in Rome or Marco Polo Airport (VCE) in Venice.
  • Flight duration is approximately 16–18 hours with layovers.
  • Airlines include Qatar Airways, Emirates, and Etihad Airways.

Transportation

Once in Italy, travel efficiently using:

  • Trains: High-speed trains connect major cities like Rome, Venice, and Florence.
  • Metro and Buses: Convenient for navigating through cities like Rome and Milan.
  • Taxis and Ride-Sharing Apps: Ideal for short distances and local travel.

Other Must-Visit Places

Milan

Milan is Italy’s fashion and business hub, known for its elegant boutiques and the magnificent Duomo di Milano. It’s a city where modernity meets history.

Naples

Naples is famous for its vibrant atmosphere, historical landmarks, and authentic Neapolitan pizza. It’s also the gateway to exploring the stunning Amalfi Coast.
