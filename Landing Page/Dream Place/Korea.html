South Korea

Breathtaking Sceneries

Seoul, a vibrant city blending tradition and modernity
Jeju Island, famous for its natural beauty and scenic landscapes
Busan, a coastal city known for its beaches and seafood

Why South Korea Is Special To Me

I want to visit South Korea to explore its beautiful places, experience its delicious food, and see its famous tourist spots. South Korea offers a perfect combination of modern cities, historical landmarks, and natural wonders. From the bustling streets of Seoul to the peaceful beauty of Jeju Island and the lively coastal vibe of Busan, there’s so much to discover. I’m also interested in learning more about Korean culture and experiencing the warmth of the people there.

How to Get There

By Air

Major international airports:

  • Direct flights from Manila (MNL) to Incheon International Airport (ICN) or Gimhae International Airport (PUS) in Busan.
  • Flight duration is approximately 4 hours.
  • Airlines include Philippine Airlines, Cebu Pacific, and Korean Air.

Transportation

Once in Korea, travel efficiently using:

  • Subway System: Fast and efficient for navigating major cities like Seoul and Busan.
  • Buses: Ideal for traveling between cities or exploring local areas.
  • Taxis and Ride-Sharing Apps: Convenient for shorter distances and easy travel.

Other Must-Visit Places

Gyeongju

Known as the “Museum Without Walls,” Gyeongju is home to ancient relics and UNESCO World Heritage Sites that showcase Korea’s rich history.

Incheon

Incheon is a lively city with a beautiful waterfront, modern attractions, and historical sites. It’s also the gateway to exploring nearby islands.
